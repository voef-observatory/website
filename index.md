## About VOEF

The **Vietnam Observatory on Economics and Finance (VOEF)** is an academic research initiative bringing together economists and finance scholars who aim to promote highâ€‘quality, policyâ€‘relevant, and internationally visible research on economics and finance, with a special attention on the Vietnamese economy and its financial system. 

The Observatory serves as a platform for collaboration, knowledge dissemination, and engagement with policymakers, institutions, and the broader public. 

---

## Mission

- Advance high-quality research in economics and finance (not only related to Vietnam)  
- Promote collaboration among Vietnam-focused economists worldwide  
- Strengthen research partnerships with Vietnamese economists and finance scholars  
- Contribute to evidence-based policy discussions  
- Increase the global visibility of research on economics and finance in Vietnam

---

## Research Areas

- Macroeconomics and economic growth
- Public economics
- Development economics
- Agricultural economics
- Environmental economics 
- Behavioral economics 
- Trade, globalization, and industrial policy  
- Labor markets and human capital
- Finance and banking
